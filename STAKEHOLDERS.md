# Stakeholder Analysis
## Student Early Warning System

---

| Stakeholder | Role | Key Concerns | Pain Points | Success Metrics |
|------------|------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| Students | Primary users who monitor their academic progress | Want early feedback on performance and risk status | Lack of awareness of academic standing until late | Improved academic performance and early alerts |
| Lecturers | Capture attendance and grades, monitor student performance | Efficient tracking of student data and identifying at-risk students | Manual tracking is time-consuming and error-prone | Faster identification of at-risk students |
| Academic Advisors | Provide support and intervention for at-risk students | Early identification of struggling students | Delayed identification reduces intervention effectiveness | Increased student retention rates |
| System Administrator | Manages system configuration and user access | System reliability and user management | Managing multiple users manually | Smooth system operation with minimal downtime |
| IT Support Staff | Maintain system performance and technical support | System uptime and performance | System failures and maintenance complexity | Reduced downtime and faster issue resolution |
| University Management | Oversees academic performance and student success | Improved graduation and retention rates | Lack of real-time academic insights | Increased student success rate and reduced dropout rate |
