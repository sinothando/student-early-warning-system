# Reflection

During the development of this assignment, one of the main challenges was balancing the needs of different stakeholders. For example, students require quick and real-time feedback on their academic performance, while IT staff prioritize system stability and performance.

Another challenge was ensuring that the system remains simple enough for a single developer while still meeting the needs of all stakeholders. This required limiting the scope and avoiding complex features such as advanced machine learning models.

Additionally, defining clear and measurable requirements was important to avoid ambiguity. Ensuring that each requirement could be traced back to stakeholder needs helped improve the overall quality of the system design.

Overall, this assignment improved my understanding of requirements engineering and the importance of stakeholder analysis in designing effective software systems.
