# Sprint Plan
## Student Early Warning System

---

## Sprint Goal

The goal of this sprint is to implement core academic monitoring functionality, including attendance tracking, grade management, and risk detection. This contributes to the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) by enabling early identification of at-risk students and providing basic monitoring capabilities for lecturers and advisors.

---

## Selected User Stories

- US-001: View academic performance
- US-003: Record attendance
- US-004: Upload grades
- US-009: Risk detection
- US-010: Generate alerts

## Sprint Contribution to MVP

This sprint focuses on delivering the core functionality required for the system to operate effectively. By implementing attendance tracking, grade management, and risk detection, the system will be able to identify at-risk students and provide meaningful insights to lecturers and advisors.

These features form the foundation of the Student Early Warning System and ensure that the MVP delivers immediate value to stakeholders by enabling early intervention and improved academic monitoring.
